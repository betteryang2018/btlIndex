ABOUT CIRCUIT SOLUTION

14 YEARS OF PRODUCTIONEXPERIENCE

For more than decadeswe have been providing our customers worldwide with hi-quality printed circuit boards and assembly products that are widely used in various industries such as telecommunications, aerospace technology, security, medical devices, automotive control, and lighting and consumer electronics.
